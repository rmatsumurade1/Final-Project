Asian Representation in Brazilian Art

This is my project for WEBTECH

Description

AboutShowcase a portfolio and experiences of Asian-Brazilian artists in the Brazilian arts industry
ObjectiveDemonstrate the diversity of life experiences and influences from a varied range of fellow artists
Target AudiencePeople interested in arts, ethnical discussions, and who want to know who to learn more.

Planned Content

Testimonials

In this page, I want to gather quotes and narrations on the experience of being an Asian-Brazilian artist amid COVID-19

Yellow-Brazilians You Need to Know

For this, I want to list a few Yellow-Brazilian artists and showcase their portfolio and social media profiles

Brown-Brazilians You Need to Know

Besides listing a few Brown-Brazilian artists and showcase their portfolio and social media profiles, I also want to further a discussion on how when we think about Asians, we forget about Brown (Middle East, South Asians, etc).

How to support your local artists

And for the last page, I want to gather external pages, resources, crowdfundings, and other resources to support artistic endeavours.
