Testimonials on Representation

Why is it so important to showcase the works of Asians in Brazil?

When I started looking for Asian-Brazilian artists, I had the first opportunity to understand society as a structure beyond the Western binary of black vs white, Caucasian opposed to African, and also to see myself in there. ~woonori Taking into consideration our social, economic, and environmental models, it is of an important social impact value to both minorities as a group and minorities as individuals. It is similar to the way a black kid sees themselves in the screens when watching Black Panther in the movies, because that's when she understands "yeah, I can". ~Rubens Borges
